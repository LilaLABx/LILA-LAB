## Example

```
# LILA Lab Monthly — June 2026

Welcome to the first issue of LILA Lab Monthly. This is where we share what's happening across the LILA ecosystem — new papers, pipeline improvements, community milestones, and how you can get involved.

---

## 📄 New Research

**Technical Report #2: Systematic Review of 20 Years of Economic Narrative Indices** — submitted to arXiv. This paper replicates and extends the full ENI methodology literature from 2007 to 2025. We found that our Bangla classification results are consistent with 13 prior English-language studies, and the TF-IDF+LogReg baseline is more competitive than the literature suggests.

## 🛠️ Pipeline Updates

The BENI pipeline now has full documentation for language adaptation. The modulariation means anyone can swap the language data and run the full pipeline with 3 commands (see the tutorial in COLLABORATION.md).

## 👥 Community Highlights

This month we launched the LILA Communications Center — this repository is now the single source of truth for all LILA Lab channels (social, research platforms, community).

## 🌍 Language Spotlight

**Bangla (বাংলা)** remains our flagship pipeline. With 265M speakers, BENI is currently the only validated economic narrative index for any Bangladeshi language. Next up: Assamese.

## 📅 What's Next

- July: Paper 3 (BENI Pipeline) submission
- July: First AENI extension proposal expected
- August: Paper 4 (Nowcasting) draft

## 🤝 How to Get Involved

We're seeking Assamese, Nepali, and Sylheti native speakers for annotation work. Each contributor receives co-authorship on the resulting paper.

Full details: [COLLABORATION.md link]

---

*LILA Lab: Language Intelligence for Low-resource Applications*
```
