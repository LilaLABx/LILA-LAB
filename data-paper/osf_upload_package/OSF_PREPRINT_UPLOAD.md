## Suggested OSF Abstract

BENI v1.0 is a derived Bangla news measurement layer for economic narrative
research. It harmonises dated Potrika articles from 2014-2020 with post-2020
BNAD articles from 2021-2024, preserving source provenance, publication dates,
original and harmonised categories, text hashes, duplicate flags, and economic
seed labels. The BENI v1.0 build contains 1,467,705 dated Bangla news article
records and flags 14,195 exact duplicate rows across 11,951 duplicate groups.
The release is intended for economic text classification, Bangla NLP, narrative
index construction, and source-break diagnostics. BENI v1.0 is a measurement
layer, not a replacement for the upstream news corpora.
